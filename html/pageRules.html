RULES

PRINCIPLE

The game, electronic, illuminates one of the four colors and produces a sound always associated with this color.

The player must then press the button of the color that has just lit up in a fairly short delay.

The game repeats the same color and sound, then randomly adds a new color.

The player must reproduce this new sequence. Each time the player reproduces correctly the sequence, the game adds a new color.

GOAL

The goal of the game is to reproduce the longest sequence
